Tagging Guide

Tag what you know.

Tags may have a namespace for example "gender:female" or "creator:furball".

The default supported namespaces are:

  • creator
  • character
  • species
  • series
  • gender
  • medium
  • meta
  • rating

If you want to use a tag with ":" example ":3" enter: "::3" or "none::3"

Use newlines as delimiter

Tags may have spaces

Example tag string:

gender:female
creator:nightfaux
species:renamon
ass
my super tag
pussy
