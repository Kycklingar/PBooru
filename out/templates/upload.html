Tagging Guide

Tags may have a namespace for example "gender:female" or "creator:yawg".

The default supported namespaces are:

  • creator
  • character
  • species
  • series
  • gender

If you want to use a tag with ":" example ":3" enter: "::3" or "none::3"

Tags and namespaces may not be longer than 64 characters respectively

Use comma "," or newlines as delimiter

Tags may have spaces

Example tag string: "gender:female, creator:nightfaux, species:renamon, ass, my super tag, pussy"
